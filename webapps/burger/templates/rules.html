Your goal is to build the burger faster than your oponent. The player who places the last layer first wins the game.

At any point, the next layer is shown on the TV.



In order to place the next layer, click on the corresponding ingredient on the conveyor belt. But be quick: only one player can pick a given ingredient.

Grabbing a wrong ingredient will cause your screen to freeze, preventing you from picking an ingredient for a few seconds, so make the right choice.

Grabbing the golden spatula will serve as a magic substitute for any ingredient.
